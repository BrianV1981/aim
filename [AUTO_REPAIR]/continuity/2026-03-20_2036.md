There is also migration debt from the `forensic.db` to `engram.db` rename. The rename happened, but any scripts, hooks, docs, or agents still mentally or literally referencing “forensic” may now be drifting. The specialist architecture is scaffolded, but still thin. The sentinel hardening fixed one specific enforcement hole, but policy debt remains.
